1.1 Basics.Fonts Fonts

The standard fonts used in 20 Minutes digital projects are Open Sans and Helvetica.

Helvetica is used as the body text font.

Open Sans is included in the project and available in 3 weights: Regular, Bold and Extra Bold. Open Sans must be systematically used for content titles :

  • bold or extra bold weight, in block teaser or content view,
  • regular in listed teaser view.

The project also comes with a custom icon font. See section ‘Icon’ section for description and usages.
